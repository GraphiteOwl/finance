============================================
	Financial Modeling Research
============================================

Purpose:
Model companies to research and forecast the finanical sector of the market with 
a special interest in crypto currency

How to use:
+ Each ticker/stock/company will have its own Excel file with its respective ticker as the name (example: COIN.xlsx)

+ Download the contents of the "Finance Universe" folder.

+ Open the "Financial Universe.xlsx" file first and simply click on the hyperlinks to on the left to open the 
  individual Excel sheet for that company for a deeper dive.
